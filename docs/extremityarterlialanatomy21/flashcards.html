Question 8
Missed
Front
Exercise causes ________________ which leads to _________________ in the lower extremities.
Choices
Answer: C. vasodilation, decreased resistance
Feedback:
Vasodilation occurs and resistance to flow drops to encourage more flow volume distally, as the muscles require more oxygen with exercise. The resistance to flow decreases and pressure increases with exercise to increase the flow volume to the functioning muscles. If enough blood does not reach the muscles, symptoms like claudication can occur.
