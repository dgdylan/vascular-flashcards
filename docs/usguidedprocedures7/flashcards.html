Question 54

Which of the following is an expected finding with an AV fistula caused by an interventional procedure?
Study Mode
Not answered yet
Answer: D. direct connection between a single artery and vein
Feedback:
An AVM is a congenital malformation with multiple connections between the arterioles and venules without a capillary bed in between. Venous collaterals will develop and dilate over time. An AVF is a direct connection of an artery or vein caused by trauma or interventional procedure. Due to the acute formation of the connection, there are no collaterals present. Which of the following is an absolute contraindication to an endovenous ablation?
Study Mode

Answer: C. uncooperative patient
Feedback:
An absolute contraindication is situation which makes a particular treatment or procedure absolutely inadvisable.
